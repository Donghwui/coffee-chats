SE Coffee Chats ☕️

SE Coffee Chats is an initiative to help lower-year SEs meet upper-years by matching students to meet up virtually and in-person.

To make sure you get the most value out of your chat, tell us who you want to talk to, and we'll match you based on bandwidth.

We encourage you to meet multiple times throughout the term if your match is also interested (ask them).
