SE Coffee Chats ☕️

About

SE Coffee Chats is an initiative to help new SE students meet upper year students. The goal is to help you get to know upper years better, learn something new, and make a new friend!

To make sure you get the most value out of your chat, you tell us who you want to grab coffee with, and we'll match you based on bandwidth and first come first serve. Once you get matched, you'll submit a form to Shaz, pick up a $10 Balzac's gift card, and have your chat!
